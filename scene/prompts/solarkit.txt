Props

Name: Bare Delta Chassis
image prompt:
Technical product render of a delta utility vehicle chassis constructed from silver anodized 4040 aluminum T-slot extrusions forming a trapezoidal passenger area tapering toward a single front steering neck mount. Reinforced aluminum gussets secure all joints with visible M8 fasteners. The chassis includes lower mounting rails for battery installation and rear drivetrain supports. No steering components, wheels, suspension, brakes, electronics, canopy supports or solar panel installed. White seamless background. Orthographic 3/4 perspective.

Name: Steering Chassis
image prompt:
Technical product render of the delta chassis with a complete scooter front assembly installed. A telescopic scooter fork with integrated steering neck bearings supports a 14-inch scooter wheel fitted with a utility tire and hydraulic disc brake. Motorcycle-style handlebars with thumb throttle, dual brake controls, reverse switch and voltmeter display are mounted to the fork. Rear drivetrain, battery, electronics enclosure, canopy supports and solar panel are not installed. White seamless background. Orthographic 3/4 perspective.
